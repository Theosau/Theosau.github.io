I am an Advanced Technologies & Research Engineer at Arup, where I work on machine learning, fluid dynamics and sustainability. My journey through mechanical engineering and computer science has taught me a lot about the power of combining these fields to solve real-world problems. It's about making software that not only thinks but also understands the physical world it operates in. This approach, guided by first principles reasoning, has been at the heart of my projects.
