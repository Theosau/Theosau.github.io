I am a PhD sutdent in Mechanical Engineering at UC Berkeley, where I work on Physics Informed Machine Learning (ML) and Accelerating Physics Numerical Simulations with ML, advised by Philip Marcus. I have 4+ years of demonstrated experience in programming, building ML, deep learning, and symbolic AI models for various applications such as computer vision, physical modeling and behaviour understanding (in criminology).

Prior to joinging Berkeley, I recieved an MEng in Mechanical Engineering and an MSc in Computing (Specialism AI and ML), both from Imperial College London. During these, I had the chance of collaborating with researchers such as Salvador Navarro-Martinez, Alessandra Russo, and Dalal Alrajeh.

I have industrial experience gained in different tech-focused start-ups. I applied ML to supply chain challenges at Vekia, and to quality control and anomaly detection on images at Scortex.
