Undergraduate Teaching Assistant, Department of Mechanical Engineering

Imperial College London, UK, 2018-2019

Led weekly tutorials for a group of 15 first and second year students to develop their understanding of technical concepts in: Mathematics (Vector Calculus, Partial Differential Equations, Fourier Transforms and Series, Matrix Algebra, Numerical Methods, Probability & Statistics), Fluid Dynamics (Incompressible Flows, Turbulence, Compressible Flows), and Thermodynamics (1st and 2nd Laws, Introduction to Combustion).
